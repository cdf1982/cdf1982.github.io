My personal celebration has been shipping _[a few good updates]({{ site.baseurl }}/glancecam/glancecam-release-notes)_ recently, plus the significant and progress I'm making with the new GlanceCam app for iPhone, iPad, Apple Watch and Apple TV that will arrive as a separate product later this year.

To the next 100 ratings, I'll do my best to make them all 5 stars!
